5-Day Budget Itinerary: Coorg, Ooty & Mysore

Starting from Bengaluru on 29/09/2025

Day 1: Bengaluru → Mysore
  • 🚍 Take bus/cab: RedBus / AbhiBus
  • 🕘 9:00 AM – Departure from Bengaluru
  • 12:30 PM – Check-in: Taj Lalitha Mahal Palace (or budget stay)
  • 2:00 PM – Visit Mysore Palace
  • 5:00 PM – Brindavan Gardens (evening show)
  • Overnight stay in Mysore
Day 2: Mysore → Coorg
  • 🚍 Private cab or KSRTC bus: KSRTC
  • 🕘 8:00 AM – Depart Mysore
  • 11:30 AM – Check-in: Taj Madikeri Resort
  • 1:00 PM – Visit Abbey Falls
  • 3:00 PM – Raja’s Seat
  • 6:00 PM – Madikeri Fort
  • Overnight stay in Coorg
Day 3: Coorg Sightseeing
  • 🕘 8:00 AM – Dubare Elephant Camp
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch & Coffee Plantation Tour
  • 3:00 PM – Omkareshwara Temple
  • Evening leisure at resort
  • Overnight stay in Coorg
Day 4: Coorg → Ooty
  • 🚖 Book cab (recommended due to hills) via Uber or local operator
  • 🕘 7:00 AM – Depart Coorg
  • 12:00 PM – Check-in: Savoy Ooty
  • 2:00 PM – Ooty Botanical Garden
  • 4:00 PM – Ooty Lake & Boating
  • Overnight stay in Ooty
Day 5: Ooty Sightseeing → Bengaluru
  • 🕘 8:00 AM – Doddabetta Peak
  • 11:00 AM – Tea Factory visit
  • 2:00 PM – Lunch
  • 4:00 PM – Depart for Bengaluru (overnight bus or cab)
  • Bus booking: RedBus Ooty → Bengaluru
