Full itinerary with timings & notes

Day 1 — Bengaluru → Mysore

Arrive by ~09:00. Visit Mysore Palace (10:00–12:00), St. Philomena's Church, lunch near Devaraja Market, Chamundi Hill afternoon, Brindavan Gardens in the evening for lights/fountain.

Day 2 — Mysore → Coorg

Leave by 08:00 by cab, arrive ~12:00. Afternoon: Madikeri Fort, Raja's Seat at sunset, plantation walk.

Day 3 — Coorg → Ooty

Start by 11:00 for the long hill drive; expect 6–7 hours depending on stops. Arrive evening and rest.

Day 4 — Ooty

Doddabetta early morning, Botanic Gardens, Ooty Lake. Option: half-day trip to Coonoor (Sim's Park).

Day 5 — Return

Take overnight Volvo and reach Bengaluru next morning/afternoon.
