5-Day Budget Itinerary: Mysore, Kabini, Coorg & Ooty

Starting from Bengaluru on 29/09/2025

Day 1: Bengaluru → Mysore (29 Sep 2025)
  • 🚍 Take bus/cab: RedBus / AbhiBus
  • 🗺 Google Maps: Bengaluru → Mysore (~145 km, 3h 30m)
  • 🕘 10:00 AM – Departure from Bengaluru
  • 1:30 PM – Check-in: Royal Orchid Metropole or Lalitha Mahal Palace
  • 🍴 Lunch: RRR Mysore or Mylari
  • 3:30 PM – Visit Mysore Palace
  • 6:30 PM – Brindavan Gardens (light & fountain show)
  • 🚖 Private Cab: Savaari / Ola Outstation
  • Overnight stay in Mysore
Day 2: Mysore → Kabini (30 Sep 2025)
  • 🗺 Google Maps: Mysore → Kabini (~60 km, 1h 30m)
  • 🕘 9:00 AM – Depart Mysore
  • 11:00 AM – Check-in: Kabini River Lodge or Evolve Back Kabini
  • 🍴 Lunch at resort
  • 3:00 PM – Jeep Safari at Nagarhole National Park
  • 6:00 PM – Sunset boat ride on Kabini River
  • Overnight stay at Kabini
Day 3: Kabini → Coorg (1 Oct 2025)
  • 🗺 Google Maps: Kabini → Coorg (~120 km, 3h 30m)
  • 🕘 8:00 AM – Depart Kabini
  • 12:00 PM – Check-in: Taj Madikeri Resort or Evolve Back Coorg
  • 🍴 Lunch: Coorg Cuisine
  • 2:00 PM – Visit Abbey Falls
  • 4:00 PM – Raja’s Seat
  • 6:00 PM – Madikeri Fort
  • Overnight stay in Coorg
Day 4: Coorg → Ooty (2 Oct 2025)
  • 🚖 Recommended cab via Savaari / Ola Outstation
  • 🗺 Google Maps: Coorg → Ooty (~240 km, 6h)
  • 🕘 7:00 AM – Depart Coorg
  • 1:00 PM – Check-in: Savoy Ooty or Fortune Sullivan Court
  • 🍴 Lunch: Earl’s Secret
  • 2:30 PM – Ooty Botanical Garden
  • 4:30 PM – Ooty Lake & Boating
  • Overnight stay in Ooty
Day 5: Ooty Sightseeing → Bengaluru (3 Oct 2025)
  • 🕘 8:00 AM – Doddabetta Peak
  • 11:00 AM – Tea Factory visit
  • 🍴 Lunch: Nahar Sidap Paradise
  • 2:00 PM – Free time / shopping
  • 4:00 PM – Depart for Bengaluru (overnight bus or cab)
  • 🚌 Bus booking: RedBus Ooty → Bengaluru
  • 🚖 Private Cab: Savaari
  • 🗺 Google Maps: Ooty → Bengaluru (~270 km, 6h 30m)
